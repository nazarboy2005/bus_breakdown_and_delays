# 🚍 NYC Bus Transportation Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **bus transportation data** from the **New York Division of Transportation** to identify key patterns in **delays and breakdowns**. The goal is to provide actionable insights to improve the efficiency and reliability of the city's bus system.

## 📂 Dataset Structure
This project is stored in a **single Excel file** with multiple sheets:

- **Scenario** – Project background and research questions.
- **Bus_Breakdown_and_Delays_NYC** – Raw data with bus delay and breakdown records.
- **CLEANED DATA** – Processed dataset for analysis.
- **Question 1** – Analysis of most common reasons for delays and breakdowns.
- **Question 2** – Breakdown of delay times by borough and bus company.
- **Question 3** – Correlation analysis between delays/breakdowns and weekdays.

Simply open the **dataset.xlsx** file and navigate through the sheets to explore the analysis.

## 🔎 Key Insights
### **1️⃣ What are the most common reasons for delays and breakdowns?**
- 🚦 **Heavy Traffic** accounted for **62%** of total delays causes.
- 🔧 **Mechanical Problems** were the leading cause of breakdowns (**10,399 incidents recorded**).
- ✅ **Recommendation**: Schedule buses in non-peak hours and increase preventive maintenance.

### **2️⃣ How do delay times vary by bus company and borough?**
- 📍 **Manhattan, Rockland County, and Queens** had the highest delay times.
- 🏢 **MAR-CAN Transport, LORINDA Enterprises, and MUT Bus Company** had the longest delays.
- ✅ **Recommendation**: Prioritize operational efficiency in highly congested boroughs and review performance of bus companies.

### **3️⃣ Is there a correlation between days of the week and breakdowns or delays?**
- 📅 **Monday had the highest number of delays and breakdowns**, with incidents decreasing toward Friday.
- ✅ **Recommendation**: Schedule **bus maintenance over weekends** to minimize breakdowns on Mondays.

## 📊 Data Visualization
The project includes:
- **Bar Charts & Pie Charts**: Showing breakdown and delay reasons.
- **Line Graphs**: Visualizing weekday trends in delays and breakdowns.
- **Tables & Summaries**: Highlighting key statistics.
